Unit 19 — Triangles

Triangles are among the most tested figures on the SAT.
Key properties involve angle sums, types of triangles, and similarity rules.

19.1 Isosceles and Equilateral Triangles

  • Isosceles Triangle: 2 equal sides → 2 equal angles.
  • Equilateral Triangle: All sides equal, all angles =60∘.

Example 1
If an isosceles triangle has vertex angle 40∘, each base angle is:

180−402=70∘

19.2 Right Triangles

A right triangle has one 90∘ angle.
Pythagorean theorem:

a2+b2=c2

where c is the hypotenuse.

Common triples: (3,4,5), (5,12,13), (7,24,25), (8,15,17).

19.3 Special Right Triangles

45-45-90 Triangle

  • Legs equal.
  • Hypotenuse =leg·2.

30-60-90 Triangle

  • Short leg =x.
  • Hypotenuse =2x.
  • Long leg =x3.

Example 2
In a 30-60-90 triangle, if the hypotenuse is 10, then:
- Short leg =5
- Long leg =53

19.4 Similar Triangles

Triangles are similar if their angles are equal and sides proportional.

a1a2=b1b2=c1c2

Example 3
If △ABC~△DEF, and AB=4, BC=6, EF=9, find DE.

Scale factor =69=23.
So DE=AB·32=4·32=6.

19.5 Radians

Radians measure angles in terms of arc length.

180∘=π radians

Conversions:
- Degrees → radians: multiply by π/180
- Radians → degrees: multiply by 180/π

Example 4
Convert 120∘ to radians:

120∘·π180=2π3

19.6 SAT Tips

  • Always check if triangle is special (30-60-90 or 45-45-90).
  • Use Pythagorean triples to save time.
  • Mark equal sides/angles in isosceles problems.
  • Watch for hidden similar triangles (especially in geometry diagrams).

Practice Problems (Unit 19)

1) Find the missing angle in a triangle with angles 40∘ and 75∘.
2) In an isosceles triangle, the vertex angle is 100∘. Find the base angles.
3) A right triangle has legs 9 and 12. Find the hypotenuse.
4) In a 45-45-90 triangle, the hypotenuse is 8. Find the legs.
5) Convert 225∘ into radians.
6) Two similar triangles have sides 3,4,5 and 6,x,10. Find x.

Solutions

1) 180−40−75=65∘.

2) Base angles =(180−100)/2=40∘.

3) c=92+122=81+144=225=15.

4) Leg =8/2=42.

5) 225∘·π/180=5π4.

6) Scale factor =2. So x=8.
